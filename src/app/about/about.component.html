About AcademixConnect!

An AcademixConnect website designed for educational institutions, with features for students and teachers to create accounts, update profiles, and maintain data, can provide a comprehensive platform for efficient academic management. Here's a brief description of the key functionalities:

  • Account Creation:
    Student Registration : New students can create accounts by providing essential details such as name, email, contact information, and enrollment details.
    Teacher Registration : Educators can register by submitting information including their name, email, subject expertise, and other relevant details.
  • Dashboard :
    Upon login, users are greeted with a personalized dashboard displaying relevant information, announcements, and quick links.
  • Profile Management :
    Student Profiles : Students can update their personal details, including contact information, academic records, and additional information.
  • Academic Records :
    Student Academic History : Students can view and track their academic progress, including grades, courses completed, and ongoing classes.
    Teacher Course Management : Educators can manage and update information related to the courses they teach,
  • Attendance Tracking :
    Teachers can mark and manage student attendance, and students can view their attendance records.
  • Event Management :
    Information about upcoming events, examinations, and important dates are displayed, allowing users to stay informed.
